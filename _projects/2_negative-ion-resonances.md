I work on methods for temporary anion states and related resonance phenomena, including the role of exact exchange and dynamical long-range polarization in metastable electronic structure.

These problems sit at the intersection of formal theory and spectroscopy-driven applications, making them a useful testing ground for method development that still has clear physical interpretation.
