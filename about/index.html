About

I am currently a PhD scholar in the Quantum Chemistry Group at Tata Institute of Fundamental Research. I have listed some of my research interests below. Apart from my work, I enjoy playing table tennis, go for hikes, and treks. You can read about some of the interesting ones in my blogs. I am also interested in learning programming languages like Python, C++, HTML5, CSS3, and Bash.

Research Interests

  • Development of quantum chemical methods beyond the random phase approximation (RPA) within the density functional theory (DFT) framework
  • Theory of metastable and driven systems
  • Inter/intra-molecular interactions

Educational Background

  • August 2018 - present : Graduate student, TIFR, Mumbai.
  • July 2016 - May 2018 : Masters in Chemistry, University of Delhi, New Delhi.
  • July 2013 - May 2016 : Bachelors (with Honors) in Chemistry, St. Stephen’s College, New Delhi.

Previous Work Experience

  • May 2017 - July 2017 : IAS-INSA-NASI Summer Research Fellow in Molecular Modelling Group, Indian Institute of Chemical Technology, Hyderabad. I worked towards understanding Big Data driven approaches in chemistry/cheminformatics with Prof. G. N. Sastry.

  • September 2015 - May 2016 : Delhi University’s Innovation Project (SSC-301) which included ‘In-silico and in-vitro studies of Begomovirus sp. affecting dicotyledous plants in the Indian sub-continent’.
